Overview

A large part of my role as a Solution Sales Engineer is to not only understand the ICONICS technology but also be able to explain the software and its features to a non-technical audience. The ability to articulate these technical concepts is one of my professional strengths. This communication has taken several forms over my time at ICONICS ranging from blog posts, training videos, webcasts, and other events.
